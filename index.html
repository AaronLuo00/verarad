Why now

Static benchmarks miss clinical readiness.

Radiology AI fails in the workflow gaps: context use, prior comparison, urgency, and report quality.

Real radiology is a workflow, not an image caption.

Deployment depends on clinical indication, prior comparison, urgency, uncertainty, and reporting style. Single-image scores rarely expose the failures that matter in practice.

Model teams need failures they can train on.

A useful evaluation should not end with a leaderboard score. It should identify dangerous misses and package corrected examples, critiques, and regression cases for the next training run.

Platform

Private evals that become a post-training data engine.

We structure radiology cases as workflow tasks, score model behavior with clinical rubrics, and turn repeated failure modes into training assets your team can use.
