Why now

Radiology AI is moving from image models to clinical agents.

Static benchmarks miss the deployment blockers.

Radiology deployment depends on context use, prior comparison, uncertainty, urgency handling, and report quality. A single image-to-answer score rarely exposes those failure modes.

Model teams need failures they can train on.

A useful evaluation should not end with a leaderboard score. It should identify dangerous failures and package corrected examples, critiques, and regression cases for post-training.

Platform

Private evals that become a training data engine.

We structure radiology cases as workflow tasks, score model behavior with clinical rubrics, and turn repeated failure modes into training assets your team can use.
